## Essentials Only

If you are building a Stringman Arpeggio and want to source the common parts yourself — motors, Pis, cameras, servos, fasteners, and so on — this kit covers the parts that are not easy to buy off the shelf.

The full Bill of Materials is published in the [Stringman Arpeggio BOM](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bZy7uijzaD2q74BN9S1-xETvV-d4Wz9Zg1J2bpjwM2k/edit?gid=204754068#gid=204754068).

## What's Included

- 2 Neufangled Arpeggio Anchor Hats (PCBs)
- 1 Stringman Gripper Hat (PCB)
- 7m FEP-jacketed power tether line
- 1 rangefinder wire harness
- 1 gripper pole power wire

## What You Need to Provide

Everything else in the [Arpeggio BOM](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bZy7uijzaD2q74BN9S1-xETvV-d4Wz9Zg1J2bpjwM2k/edit?gid=204754068#gid=204754068), plus the 3D printed parts from our [downloads page](/docs/downloads/).

In the United States, the links in the BOM are mostly for Ali Express and Amazon.

If you would rather buy the complete hardware bundle, see the [Stringman Hardware Kit](/products/stringman-hardware-kit/).
