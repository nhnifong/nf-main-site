## Material

Tripod mounts for Stringman printed in PETG-CF with heat set inserts and alconite rings.

## Use

In addition to attaching the mounts to tripods, the tripods must be securly held down with ample weight and guy wires.
Tie off points on the back of the mounts can be used with a knot such as a buntline hitch to attach guy wires to weights such as water bags.
