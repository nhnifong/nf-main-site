## Example Issues

- **Mechatronic Design:** Select the right motors, sensors, controllers, and manufacturing process to design a weatherproof sign that can raise and lower automatically.
- **Software Integration:** Re-package a Raspberry Pi based system as a custom image so it can be quickly deployed to more machines.
- **Machine Learning:** Help me get started with imitation learning and figure out how it can be applied to automate a task in my business, whether with a Stringman robot or other commercial robot.
- **Hybrid Vibe Coding:** Teach me how to produce more effective, clean, and reliable systems with help from AIs. What is the best approach for collaboratively designing and improving a large system?
- **Full Stack Web Deployment:** Explain how to transform my website into something that can accommodate a complex interactive application deployed in Google Cloud.
- **Professional Documentation:** Show me how to get started with MkDocs and have it deployed to my website automatically.

## How it Works

Once you check out, you'll receive a link to book your sessions via Google Meet. You can use your three hours all at once for a deep dive, or spread them out over multiple weeks as your project progresses.
