Stringman

Cable-driven room-scale organization robot

learn more

Room-Scale Reach

Stringman is hanging gripper that can reach the entire volume of your room. It identifies objects on the floor such as laundry and takes them to marked drop points such as the laundry hamper. Designed for rooms ranging from 10ft to 35ft on a side.

Autonomous Operation

Designed to operate while you're out, Stringman can perform a cleaning cycle in about 30 minutes so you can return to clear floors or schedule your robotic vacuum to follow up. As soon as it's done, it parks itself out of reach.

Open Source

Stringman is completely open source and certified by OSHWA. And complete visual guides are available if you want to print and build your own.

Remote Management

Ever needed to reach through your security camera, grab something and put it somewhere? That capability is now within reach. Either select targets and drop points, or drive it manually through the web interface.

Constantly Improving Models

Model updates are offered regularly for recognizing a greater variety of targets and obstacles and handling edge cases in new enviroments. If you're up for it, you can program your own behaviors or fine tune our models on your household data. No matter how advanced the AI gets, our belief is that tension is all you need.
